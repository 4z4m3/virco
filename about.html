VIRCO

Elevate Your Coginitive Horizon

Virco (Virtual Cognitive) is a website designed to help users overcome social media addiction by engaging them in brain training exercises. Through interactive activities and personalized cognitive challenges, Virco promotes healthier habits for a balanced mind.

"Virco is a website that I created because I saw the current condition of the young generation who are very addicted to social media. I hope Virco can help improve the cognitive abilities of the younger generation in the future"

"I see Virco as an opportunity for the younger generation to become a more prosperous and advanced generation, with brain abilities that are still very strong. I hope that the results of the work I do can be useful for the cognitive abilities of the entire younger generation."
